Web Design and SEO in Barcelona: My Work Approach

My journey in the digital world hasn't been a straight line, and that is exactly what defines my work approach today. My time at a Barcelona-based agency gave me the opportunity to work within a technical team developing sites with WordPress and Elementor Pro for a wide range of clients. There, I learned that a website shouldn't just be visually attractive; it must follow a professional methodology: being fast, secure, and built for real customer conversion.

Throughout my career, I have managed SEO and content strategies, having published over 120 optimized articles that help businesses climb Google rankings organically. This background allows me to understand the web as a whole: from clean code in HTML and CSS to Local SEO strategies that ensure a business appears exactly when someone searches for their services in their city. Furthermore, I enhance organic visibility by managing Google Business Profile, creating strategic posts with focused CTAs designed to turn searches into direct inquiries and leads.

Today, through CC Designs, I apply all that background to offer a close and transparent service. I love getting involved in every project to transform my clients vision into a digital tool that truly delivers results for them.
